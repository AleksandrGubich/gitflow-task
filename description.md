\## Name

\*\*Apple iPhone 15 (Standard Edition)\*\*



This is the standard flagship smartphone released by Apple in 2023. It combines a refreshed design with contoured edges, the introduction of the USB-C port replacing Lightning, and advanced image processing technologies previously available only in the Pro versions.



\## Description

The iPhone 15 represents a significant step forward in the base model lineup. The main innovation is the removal of the notch in favor of the Dynamic Island cutout, which interactively displays notifications and background activities. The back panel features color-infused glass with a unique matte finish. The smartphone runs on the A16 Bionic chip, ensuring high performance in gaming and apps, as well as all-day power efficiency. The switch to USB-C opens up new possibilities for charging and connecting accessories.
